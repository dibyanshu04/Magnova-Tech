Student Internship Program

In today’s job market, many fresh Engineers find it very difficult to find jobs because of lack of work experience and industrial skill sets. Magnova Engineers tries to bridge this gap between industry and academics through its Student Internship Program. We select the candidates based on their academic records, fundamental knowledge about key subjects, aptitude and communication skills. Selected candidate are offered live projects to work on for a period of 3 to 6 Months as per their college / university requirements. Many ME & MTech students have benefited from our internship program. Students undergo industrial oriented training programs and at the same time they work on live projects. At the end of the internship program, we offer them Certificate of Experience to fulfill their academic requirements. Placement assistance is also offered to these students based on their performance.

Students interested in our internship program are requested to send their updated profile to (Subject Line should be: Application for Student Internship Program): info@magnovatech.com
